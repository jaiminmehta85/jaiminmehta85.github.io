Mile Track Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Mile Track collects GPS location data to track trip distances. This data is stored only on your device and never sent to any server.

2. How We Use Information

Location data is used only to calculate mileage and IRS deductions. We do not sell or share your data with anyone.

3. Third Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob for advertisements. Google may collect device information as described at https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app deletes all data permanently.
